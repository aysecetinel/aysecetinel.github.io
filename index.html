Ayşe Çetinel

Assistant Professor of Operations Management

Faculty of Business · Özyeğin University

I am an Assistant Professor of Operations Management at Özyeğin University. My research addresses challenges at the marketing-operations interface, with a focus on how retailers and online platforms transform through omnichannel strategies, digitalization, AI, and sustainability initiatives. I employ data-driven methods including big data analytics, econometrics, quasi-experimental and field experimental designs, machine learning, and artificial intelligence to generate insights that inform strategic decision-making.

Prior to academia, I spent over a decade in the U.S. retail and e-commerce sectors, holding key strategic roles at Fortune 500 companies including Williams-Sonoma and Shutterfly.com. I hold a B.Sc. in Finance from The Ohio State University, an M.Sc. in Big Data and Business Analytics from Istanbul Technical University, and a Ph.D. in Operations Management and Information Systems from Koc University. I have held a visiting position at Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University.

Omnichannel Retail Analytics

Store Labor Optimization

Corporate Training

I offer customized corporate training programs that bring academic rigor and practical insight together. Drawing on my research in operations management, data analytics, and AI, and over a decade of industry experience in retail and e-commerce, these sessions are designed to equip teams with actionable frameworks and skills.

Training Programs

Data-Driven Decision Making

An introduction to using data analytics and quantitative methods for strategic business decisions. Covers foundational concepts in descriptive analytics, experimentation, and interpreting results, tailored for managers and non-technical teams.

AI and Prompt Engineering for Business

Hands-on training in leveraging generative AI tools effectively. Participants learn how to write high-quality prompts, integrate AI into workflows, and critically evaluate AI outputs, applicable across functions including operations, marketing, and finance.

Omnichannel Retail Strategy

An in-depth look at how physical and digital retail channels interact, based on current academic research and industry practice. Suitable for retail managers, e-commerce teams, and strategy leaders navigating digital transformation.

Operations Management Fundamentals

A practical overview of core operations concepts including process design, capacity planning, supply chain basics, and performance measurement, designed for professionals looking to strengthen their operational toolkit.
